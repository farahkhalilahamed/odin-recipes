Butter Chicken

Description

A popular Indian curry with a spiced tomato and butter gravy base. It is often enjoyed with naan or rice.

This recipe is courtesy of allrecipes.com

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 onion, minced
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 15 oz tomato sauce
  • 3 cups heavy cream
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon ceyenne pepper
  • 1 teapsone garam masala
  • 1.5 lb boneless chicken
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons tandoori masala

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 F
  2. Over medium heat, melt 2 tablespoons of butter
  3. Add onion and garlic, cooking slowly until onion caramelizes
  4. In another pan, add heavy cream, tomato sauce, salt, ceyenne powder, garam masala, and rest of the butter; mix over medium heat
  5. Reduce heat and allow it to simmer; add in caramelized onions while stirring occasionally
  6. Toss chicken with tandoori masala and vegetable oil, then spread on a baking sheet
  7. Bake chicken in oven for about 12 minutes
  8. Add cooked chicken to sauce and let it simmer
  9. Top with some more cream and serve
