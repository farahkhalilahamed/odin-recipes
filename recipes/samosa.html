Samosa

Description

A savory fried pastry with various types of filling, including potatoes, onions, or meat. They are often eaten with mint or tamarind chutney.

This recipe is courtesy of Recipe Tin Eats

Ingredients

  • 2 medium potatoes
  • Ajwain seeds
  • Flour
  • Chili
  • Frozen peas
  • Ghee or oil
  • Coriander
  • Spices

Steps

Potato Filling

  1. Boil potatoes until tender, then roughly mash with a fork
  2. To a pan, add spices, ginger, peas, and chilli to cook
  3. Add mashed potato and gently mix
  4. Top with coriander
  5. Let the stuffing fully cool before using

Samosa

  1. Mix dry ingredients
  2. Add water and stir to form soft dough, then let it sit for 30 minutes
  3. After 30 minutes, divide dough into 6 pieces
  4. Shape dough into balls
  5. Roll out each ball into circles until it's 20mm thick
  6. Cut one of the circles in half, then brush some water along the edge
  7. Fold sides of half-circle in to form a cone
  8. Stuff potato filling inside
  9. Brush water along open edge and seal the samosa cone
  10. Trim any excess dough if needed
  11. Fry until golden brown and crispy
  12. Enjoy!
