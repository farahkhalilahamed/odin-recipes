Samosa

Description

An aromatic mixed rice dish generally made with rice, spices, and meat cooked in layers. It has different variations across regions of South Asia, each with its own flavors and unique characteristics.

This recipe is courtesy of Cook with Kushi

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 400g chicken thighs
  • 1 lemon
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 6 strands of coriander leaves
  • 1/4 cup mint leaves
  • 1.5 tablespoon oil
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Wash and soak rice for 15 mins
  2. Add rice, water, and salt to a pot and cook until rice is 80% cooked
  3. Drain the rice and keep it aside
  4. In a bowl, add chicken thighs, turmeric and chili powder, salt and lemon juice. Mix and allow it to marinate for 15 mins
  5. In a pan, add oil, cinnamon sticks, cloves, cardomom, bay leaves, and cumin seeds, then sautee
  6. Add the marinated chicken and allow it to cook for 3 mins
  7. Add garlic, ginger, and green chilis and cook for 2 mins
  8. Add onion, tomato, coriander, mint, and tandoori masala; mix well
  9. Spread the masala over the base of the pan evenly before adding in rice
  10. Garnish with some more mint and coriander
  11. Sprinkle some tandoori masala powder and add 1/4 cup of water
  12. Cook on low flame until rice is cooked thoroughly
